About

I am a Research Scientist at Meta, where I work on modeling how humans appear and behave in 3D — including pose, shape, clothing, and scene interactions. I completed my PhD at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, Germany, working with Prof. Gerard Pons-Moll.

Broadly, my research focuses on building digital agents — both humans and robots — that can understand and interact with their environments in realistic and meaningful ways.
